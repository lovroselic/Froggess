About:

Frogess is a pretty obvious clone of the game Frogger (Sega, 1981). C64 had a lot of ports/clones, but major ones were from Sierra and another from Parker brothers.

In the era when games were pretty violent, demolishing aliens left and right, swallowing dots and such, this cute gem remains and epithome of peace, love and happines. Well, that is if the titular character does not ge hit by a bus.

So after Haunting the Hauntessa and DownHeel, both very complex endeavors, what motivated me do do a clone of such a simple game?
